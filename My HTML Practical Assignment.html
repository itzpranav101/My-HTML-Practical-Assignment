Some points to remember while going on beach vacation
  • To prepare for a beach trip, pack your bathing suit and a change of clothes so you don't have to stay wet and sandy on the drive home.
  • Additionally, take sun protection such as sunscreen and a hat, as well as a towel or chair to sit on.
Things you should bring before going to your beach vacation
  1. Sun cream.

  2. After Sun lotion.

  3. Hair conditioner for hair that is left under the sun, to protect your hair from the sun and saltwater.

  4. Swim brief / Swimsuit / Swim diapers.

  5. Beach towel/Picnic blanket.

  6. Sun hat/cap.

  7. Sunglasses

  8. Beach Bag
